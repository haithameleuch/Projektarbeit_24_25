Detailed Description

Defines a summoner-type enemy agent (Spawnling) trained with Unity ML-Agents.

Core Behavior:

  • Pursues the player using Rigidbody-based forward/backward movement and rotation.
  • Periodically spawns a designated prefab at its current position to increase threat or support allies.
  • Uses coroutine-based player detection and modular spawn system.

Observations:

  • Direction to the player, normalized (Vector3)
  • Agent's forward direction (Vector3)
  • Signed angle to the player, normalized [-1,1] (float)

Total = 7 observations per step.

Rewards:

  • Positive reward for reducing distance to the player.
  • Positive reward for facing the player.
  • Negative reward for being stuck or colliding with walls.

Key Features:

  • Rigidbody-based movement and rotation with frozen Y-axis position and X/Z rotation.
  • Spawn timer controlling instantiation of prefab objects.
  • Stuck detection using position delta over time.
  • Modular and adaptable for swarm or reinforcement-based enemy behaviors.

Best suited for:

  • Enemies that grow in threat over time (e.g., spawners or breeders).
  • Cooperative enemy behaviors where spawned prefabs act as reinforcements or distractions.
  • Training scenarios combining pursuit and spawning mechanics.
